Jerm

Who?
  • Jerm is a nickname I was given by my family and friends separately but equally.
  • My family felt the pronunciation of Jeremy was cumbersome, especially with most of the family members having English as a second language. At one point I was Jerms, and then they dropped the s and I became Jerm.
  • My friends just liked to give me nicknames with a J pronounced at the beginning. I was Jerome before becoming Jerm.
